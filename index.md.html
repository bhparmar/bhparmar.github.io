Bhavesh Parmar

Assistant Professor · Biomedical Engineering ·
L. D. College of Engineering, Ahmedabad

I work at the intersection of biomedical engineering, AI, biomechanics, and medical imaging, developing intelligent systems for Medical Device development, clinical diagnostics, and assistive healthcare. My research focuses on translating machine learning innovations into practical tools that enhance decision-making and improve patient outcomes.

Working at the intersection of biomedical engineering, AI, biomechanics, and medical imaging, focusing on intelligent diagnostic systems, assistive technologies, and clinically meaningful imaging solutions. My interests include brain tumor segmentation, non-invasive vital-sign monitoring, optical and digital sensing, and affordable, standards-aligned medical devices. I aim to create engineering innovations that enhance clinical decision-making, accessibility, and patient safety—through AI-driven imaging workflows, translational prototypes, and mentoring high-impact teams. Ultimately, I strive to advance equitable, reliable healthcare technologies that can be deployed in real-world settings and help shape future standards and policy.
