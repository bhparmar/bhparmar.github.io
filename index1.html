Bhavesh Parmar

Assistant Professor · Biomedical Engineering ·
L. D. College of Engineering, Ahmedabad

I work at the intersection of biomedical engineering, AI, biomechanics, and medical imaging, developing intelligent systems for Medical Device development, clinical diagnostics, and assistive healthcare. My research focuses on translating machine learning innovations into practical tools that enhance decision-making and improve patient outcomes.

I work across biomedical engineering, AI, biomechanics, and medical imaging, with a focus on designing intelligent diagnostic systems, assistive healthcare technologies, and clinically relevant imaging solutions. My broader interests span brain tumor segmentation, non-invasive vital-sign monitoring, optical and digital sensing, and the development of affordable, standards-aligned medical devices. I enjoy tackling problems where engineering innovation can directly enhance clinical decision-making, accessibility, and patient safety—whether through AI-powered imaging workflows, translational prototypes, or mentoring teams that build impactful healthcare solutions. Ultimately, my goal is to advance equitable and reliable healthcare technologies that can be deployed in real-world settings and inform future standards and policy.
